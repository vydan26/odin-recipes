Lasagna

Description

This Italian dish is made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù (ground meats and tomato sauce), béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses (which may include ricotta, mozzarella and Parmesan), and seasonings and spices.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna
  • Ragu
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Parmesan
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Layer lasagna
  2. In the oven for x hours
  3. Let cool
